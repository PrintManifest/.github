# PrintManifest

PrintManifest focuses on improving how creators publish, manage, and present 3D printable models.  
Our goal is to make the publishing experience clear, consistent, and easy to use.

---

## What We Do

PrintManifest works on the standards, workflows, and creator-facing experiences that support model publishing on our platform. This includes:

- Clear and consistent model presentation  
- Simple and predictable publishing steps  
- Helpful guidance for entering model details  
- A clean and purposeful interface for managing 3D printable content  

We focus on making the entire flow feel smooth and understandable from start to finish.
